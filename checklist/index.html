Public Pre-Launch Checklist

This checklist helps the community verify progress and avoid scams. If it’s not published on this website, it’s not official.

Identity & Official Channels

  • Official domain verified (bookmark it).
  • Official Links page published (/links).
  • Verify page published (/verify).
  • X (Twitter) and Telegram created and linked (TBA until ready).

Contract & Launch Readiness

  • Contract address published in Launch Panel + /verify (only when final).
  • DEX Pair published (only when final).
  • Liquidity / launch steps documented (internal runbook).
  • Block explorers links prepared (Etherscan, etc.).

Security & Anti-Scam Hardening

  • Single source of truth messaging on home + /links + /verify.
  • Never trust screenshots: verify domain + contract on this site.
  • Pinned posts prepared for X/Telegram pointing to /verify.

Marketing & Media

  • OG image in place (og.png) for clean social sharing.
  • Brand assets: logo, icon, banners ready.
  • First announcement thread and FAQ prepared.

Analytics & Monitoring

  • GA4 installed and receiving page_view events.
  • Dashboards/alerts prepared for traffic spikes.
